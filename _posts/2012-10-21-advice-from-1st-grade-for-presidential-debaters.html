The presidential debate is coming to Boca Raton, my hometown.  In the midst of this truly exciting event I'd just like the candidates to remember  a few ground rules that  they probably learned in 1st grade.

1. Be kind.

2. Stay in your own personal space.

3. Tell the truth.

4. Take responsibility for your actions.

5. No interrupting each other.

I could go on but 5 rules is usually the perfect number to be able to follow in 1st Grade.
